About BELMUN 2026

Discover our mission and vision for diplomatic excellence

Our Mission

BELMUN 2026 is dedicated to fostering the next generation of global leaders through the transformative experience of Model United Nations. We provide a platform where students can engage in meaningful diplomatic discourse, develop critical thinking skills, and gain a deeper understanding of international relations and global challenges.

Our conference brings together passionate individuals from diverse backgrounds to simulate the workings of the United Nations, encouraging participants to step into the shoes of diplomats and collaborate on solutions to the world's most pressing issues.

What is Model United Nations?

Model United Nations (MUN) is an educational simulation where students role-play as delegates to the United Nations and other international bodies. Participants research countries, investigate international issues, debate, deliberate, consult, and develop solutions to world problems.

Through MUN, delegates enhance their speaking, debating, and writing skills, develop critical thinking and problem-solving abilities, and gain insight into the complexities of international diplomacy.
